Introducing the Song Network

Welcome stranger! Prepare to dive into the world of songs, similar songs, and similar songs of similar songs of songs that share the same tags and use the same words. Based on the Million Song Dataset we created a network of songs, tags and words that tells a thousand stories about songs without a single sound.

95224

Songs

Songs have properties like artist, title and release year. They can have a similarity relationship between each other, which holds a score indicating how similar two songs are, if at all. Around a third of all songs have full information about tags, words, year, artist and title.

106246

Tags

Tags are user defined phrases that describe a song. These might be a genre like "rock" or descriptions of something, the tagged songs may have in common like "sad" or "title is a full sentence".

5000

Words

We are taking a network-based bag of words approach. Our database contains the 5000 most common words across the songs where lyrics are available. A song is connected to a word with the "contains" relationship that also holds the number of times the word occurs in that song.
